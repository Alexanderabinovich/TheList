4. Cyber Educational Platforms & Certification Providers

For formal training, recognized certifications, and professional development in cybersecurity:

  • Cybrary

    https://www.cybrary.it/

    Extensive free and paid courses on a wide range of cybersecurity topics.

  • EC-Council

    https://www.eccouncil.org/

    Home of the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and other cyber credentials.

  • CompTIA

    https://www.comptia.org/

    Industry‑recognized certifications such as Security+, CySA+, and PenTest+ with ample study resources.

  • GIAC

    https://www.giac.org/

    Certifications in incident response, penetration testing, digital forensics, and more.

  • Offensive Security

    https://www.offensive-security.com/

    Creators of the OSCP certification with extensive hands‑on penetration testing courses.

  • SANS Institute

    https://www.sans.org/cyber-security-courses/

    Highly regarded training with real‑world labs and certification programs.

  • (ISC)²

    https://www.isc2.Org/

    Provider of certifications like CISSP, SSCP, and more, with free exam prep resources.
